About the MMIP Cases Map Project

Learn more about our mission and goals.

The MMIP Cases Map Project is dedicated to raising awareness about the ongoing crisis of Missing and Murdered Indigenous People (MMIp). Our mission is to create a platform that visually represents the magnitude of this issue by mapping the locations of reported cases across different regions. Through this project, we hope to bring more attention to the MMIP crisis, support advocacy efforts, and contribute to the pursuit of justice.

  • To increase public awareness and understanding of the MMIP crisis.
  • To provide a centralized platform for data and resources related to MMIP cases.
  • To support advocacy efforts by offering tools for analysis and outreach.
  • To empower communities by providing a space to share their stories and demand justice.

If you're passionate about this cause and want to contribute, please contact us. We welcome volunteers, collaborators, and supporters from all walks of life.
